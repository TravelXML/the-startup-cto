FAQ

Common questions

What is a fractional CTO?
A fractional CTO provides senior technology leadership — platform architecture, strategy, technical hiring, and team mentorship — without the full-time cost. You get CTO-level thinking at 20–40% of a full-time engagement, ideal for startups and scale-ups building serious platforms.
Which industries do you specialise in?
TravelTech, RetailTech, EdTech, HealthTech, Logistics, Supply Chain, DMS/FMCG distribution, AI/GenAI platforms, and Enterprise Architecture. Over 20 years I've built and scaled platforms in all of these — so I know the domain-specific integrations, pitfalls, and patterns that generic architects miss.
Can you help with AI and GenAI platform architecture?
Yes. I work on LLM application design, RAG pipelines, agentic AI systems (LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI), fine-tuning strategies, vector database selection, MLOps pipelines, and the product and go-to-market strategy behind AI products.
How quickly can you start?
Available immediately. A free 30-minute strategy session is usually enough to determine fit and scope. Most engagements begin within a week of that first call.
Do you work with teams outside India?
Yes. I've worked on-site and remotely across India, UAE, Maldives, the US, and Canada. Async collaboration works well for most engagements; I travel for critical workshops and kick-offs.
