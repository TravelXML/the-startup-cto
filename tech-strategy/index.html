The Work

Unclear ownership, misaligned roadmaps, delivery debt compounding quietly, and engineering teams solving symptoms rather than systems. I work with founders, boards, and technology leaders to diagnose the real constraint and build the execution path that removes it. Quick answers

Is the session free?
Yes. The initial 30-minute strategy session is complimentary. I prefer to earn trust through value before discussing any ongoing engagement. If the session identifies a scope worth pursuing further, we can explore the right structure together.
What should I prepare before the call?
Come with your single most pressing technology challenge. The more specifically you can define the problem — team, timeline, constraints — the more useful our 30 minutes will be. No slides required.
Do you take on permanent CTO or VP Engineering roles?
Yes, with the right organisation and mandate. If that's the conversation you want to have, book a slot and let's see if there's a fit worth exploring.
Can you support teams outside India?
Yes. I've worked across India, UAE, Maldives, USA, and Canada, and I'm comfortable with distributed, remote-first engagements across time zones.
